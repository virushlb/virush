This brand kit defines the core visual system behind virush.lb — a creative studio focused on clean design, digital experiences and bold visuals.

Core Brand Values

  • Premium but minimal
  • Digital-first & modern
  • Creative, a bit edgy

Logos

Virush uses a clean wordmark as the primary logo and a glitch version as a display logo for louder moments.

Primary Logo

virush

The main lowercase wordmark is used on the website, proposals and small placements. It keeps the brand simple and premium.

Display Logo

VIRUSH CREATED BY VIRUSH

The glitch logo is used for covers, intros and social visuals where the brand can feel more experimental and energetic.

Usage

  • Use the primary logo for most applications and small sizes.
  • Use the glitch logo for hero images, motion graphics and thumbnails.
  • Always keep strong contrast between logo and background.

Color Palette

The Virush palette is minimal: mostly black and soft neutrals, with a warm accent inspired by product imagery.

Typography

Virush combines a bold geometric font for headings with a clean sans-serif for body copy to keep everything readable and modern.

Headlines — Montserrat

VIRUSH — PREMIUM CREATIVE STUDIO

Used for H1–H3 in bold and semibold weights. Spacing is slightly loose for a premium feel.

Body — Inter

Clean, readable text for paragraphs, captions and UI labels. Works well on both desktop and mobile.

  • Body size: 14–16px
  • Line-height: 1.5–1.7
  • Color: #111 on light backgrounds

Visual Style

The visual direction mixes product-style photography, soft light and a mix of color and black-and-white imagery. It feels like a crossover between fashion, tech and creative studio work.
